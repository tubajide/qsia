AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The Qur'an and Sunnah International Association was established for the sole purpose of engaging in the acquisition, sharing and dissemination of authentic Islamic knowledge through quiz and spelling competitions. 

The Association organizes Islamic quiz and spelling competitions among Islamic schools and Madrasah, and a special quiz competition in the blessed month of Ramadan among participants from different countries.

It also conducts online Islamic quiz on Mondays and Fridays.

The aims and objectives of the Association are as follows:

1. To be a veritable resource and platform for effective pursuit and acquisition of true Islamic knowledge. 

2. Reducing the present high level of ignorance of the Deen among Muslim Ummah through scholarly researches and dissemination of relevant knowledge of Qur'an and Sunnah.

3.  To encourage the habit of reading and the pursuit of scholarly researches among Muslim youths

4. To engage in efforts at preserving and protecting the integrity of the Qur'an and the Sunnah of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (s.a.w).

5. To aim at equipping the Muslim ummah with true Islamic knowledge for a better understanding and practice of the Deen.
